## Method tenets (Area61/BCFX)
- **"We are the indicators."** Read **market structure / price action** directly (QFs, HH/HL, break-of-structure, VWAP context); minimal-to-no lagging indicators. The human + method IS the signal.
- **Set hours.** Trade only the window (NY ~09:00–12:00 ET; funded roster 09:45–10:30) — never all day.
- **Set rules.** max trades/day, consec-loss cap, daily loss limit, the backtested DD-throttle (validated risk rule, NOT edge).
- **Selectivity / the skip is the edge:** first touch of a level is a trap; the trade is the failed-retest **re-entry** ([[feedback-qf-two-phase-premove]]). Re-enter only on a win.

## BCFX Module-7 psychology (studied 2026-07-05) — source of the Mindset track
Source: `~/code/research/bcfx-course-materials/BCFX-1.0-7-psychology.pdf` + distilled `~/code/research/bcfx-study-notes/07-psychology.md`, `00-MASTER-SYNTHESIS.md`, `10-area-61.md`. The full concept set (read these before adding Mindset lessons so nothing's missed):
1. Psychology is the only thing that beats you once the strategy works — "you're stopped out by your own psychology."
2. **Think AGAINST the masses** (central): crowd's obvious low-TF long into higher-TF supply is your short; every higher high = a better short price. The masses are the liquidity.
3. Lower-TF trap / **zoom out first**: the low-TF "breakout" rallies into major HTF support-turned-resistance and tanks. Low TFs aren't faster/safer money.
4. **Radical ownership** — "it was you, not the broker" (blaming stop-hunting/spread = timeframe blindness).
5. **Don't FREEZE after losses** — expect 2-3 stop-outs; the 4th/5th catches the runner. Freezing = the fatal error. (Reconcile: M1 = size DOWN, not stop trading; our 2-consec rail is the prop cap BCFX's retail lacked.)
6. **Let winners run** — trail past breakeven; don't cut early out of fear.
7. Don't over-leverage (small = calm psychology); confidence from repetition; it takes time (no get-rich-quick).
