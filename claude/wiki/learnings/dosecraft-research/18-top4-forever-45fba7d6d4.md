# 18-top4-forever

If you could only choose four compounds for the rest of your life — these are the four. Maximum root cause coverage. Minimum complexity. The foundation that never stops working.

Leading practitioners recommend this stack as the irreducible minimum for lifelong optimization. Every compound earns its place by addressing a distinct root cause with a unique mechanism.

Encoded in mitochondrial DNA. Activates AMPK pathway. Restores insulin sensitivity. Builds the machinery inside mitochondria. Forces fatty acid oxidation.

**Dose:** 5-10mg SubQ, 2-3x/week fasted

**Targets:** Root Cause #2 (IR) + Root Cause #3 (ATP)

Telomerase activator. Winds the clock backwards on cellular aging. Silences senescent cell inflammation. Resets circadian rhythm.

**Dose:** 500mcg-1mg/day SubQ for 10-20 days, 2-3x/year (5-10mg total per cycle)

**Targets:** Root Cause #1 (Inflammation) + Root Cause #3 (ATP)

Master regulator of VEGF repair system. Suppresses TNF-alpha, IL-6, NF-kB. Promotes IL-10. Repairs gut, brain, heart, tendons, ligaments — everything.

**Dose:** 500mcg-1mg SubQ daily (no cycling for acute injuries; 8-12wk on / 2-4wk off for extended use)

**Targets:** Root Cause #1 (Inflammation) + All Systems

Trains T-cells — doesn't boost or suppress, it recalibrates. FDA-approved as adjunctive therapy. Makes immune response smarter, not louder.

If your inflammation is severe (CRP above 3.0), do NOT add MOTS-c until BPC-157 has had time to lower baseline inflammation. MOTS-c can cause **paradoxical fatigue** if mitochondria are already inflamed — overstimulation of AMPK in damaged mitochondria triggers an oxidative burst that depletes ATP instead of building it.
