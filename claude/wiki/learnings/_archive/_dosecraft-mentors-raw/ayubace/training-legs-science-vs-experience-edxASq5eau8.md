# Training Legs: Science vs. Experience

I've never ever ever really trained my legs twice a week. Ever. Every time I'm on stage, I get complimented on my legs, my hamstrings, my quads. It's interesting because there was no science behind that. I used to just train my leg as hard as I could. I just took a full week to recover to hit them again. And they grew my best. Generally speaking, it's always one leg day a week or sometimes it's quads one day, hamstrings another
