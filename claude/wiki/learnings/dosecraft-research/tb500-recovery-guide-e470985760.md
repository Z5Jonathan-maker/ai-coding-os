Loading Phase

**4 weeks at 5mg/week.** This saturates tissue with TB-500 and initiates the full regeneration cascade. Do not skip or shorten the loading phase — it sets the foundation.

Maintenance Phase

**2.5mg/week indefinitely.** After loading, drop to half dose. This maintains tissue regeneration and flexibility gains without the higher loading dose.

Total Required (Standard)

**20mg for loading phase** (4 vials at 5mg each). Maintenance requires approximately 10mg per month (2 vials).

When to Expect Results

Most practitioners report noticeable flexibility improvements by Week 2. Significant tissue remodeling and pain reduction by Week 4. Full structural benefits by Week 6-8.

**Complete 4-6 full weeks minimum.** Pain resolution is NOT the same as tissue repair. The remodeling phase — where scar tissue reorganizes into functional tissue and collagen cross-links strengthen — continues for weeks after pain signals cease. Stopping early means incomplete healing and higher re-injury risk. Finish the full protocol.

**"This is an amplifier, not invincibility juice."** TB-500 accelerates your body's natural regeneration — it does not replace smart rehabilitation. Combine with proper rest, progressive loading, physical therapy, and mobility work. The peptide gives your body the materials; you still need to do the work. Respect the recovery timeline.
