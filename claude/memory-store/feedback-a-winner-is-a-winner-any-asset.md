universe to equity index futures (ES/NQ/YM), and do NOT treat commodity/metal/energy survivors as
second-class or "only relevant if it also transfers to indices." Commodities are fully tradeable: via
micros (MGC gold, MCL crude, MNG/QG nat gas, etc.) on cash/own-capital, and on overnight-capable prop
firms (e.g. Goat Sprint, Mon–Thu holds). Evaluate a candidate on its **edge + vessel fit**, not its ticker.

commodities. If it's a winner it's a winner." I had let "his business is index futures" artificially shrink
the opportunity set. His operation (prop evals + cash playbooks) already banks commodity edges — gold-swing
and RB gasoline seasonal are both commodity cells.
