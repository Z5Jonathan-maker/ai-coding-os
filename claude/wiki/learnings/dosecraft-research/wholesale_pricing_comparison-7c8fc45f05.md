## KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **YPB is a REAL partner** — their catalog is comprehensive, pricing is transparent, they offer both globally-sourced (cheap) and US-sourced (premium) options
2. **Membership costs ($297-497/mo) are real** — need to factor into break-even
3. **Globally Sourced is the play** — US Sourced margins are too thin at our price points
4. **Phase 1 margins (47%) are HALF of hybrid model (87%)** — but speed to market is days vs months
5. **1000+ tier (66%) is the sweet spot** before going hybrid — still uses YPB infrastructure but significantly better margins
6. **Top 5 starters at Premier pricing**: BPC-157, MOTS-c, Ipamorelin, GHK-Cu, TB-500 (all 40-62% margin)
7. **Avoid at Premier**: Selank, Semax, CJC-1295, TA-1 (negative or thin margins)
