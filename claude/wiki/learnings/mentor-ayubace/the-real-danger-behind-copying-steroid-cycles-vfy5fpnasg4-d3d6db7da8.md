# The Real Danger Behind Copying Steroid Cycles

Anabolic usage is like alcohol. Some people can drink 10 pints and work the next day. Some people drink one pint and they're on the floor. You don't know how something's going to react until it's in your body and it connects to your DNA, your genetics. Some people's bodies can take a compound like testosterone at a low dosage like 100 milligrams and they can make fantastic results from it. Other people can take the same dosage, not only not get the results, but they can get all the side effects. So, no one can really tell you what works for you. You have to experiment on yourself if you're going to go down that road at the lowest dose possible to understand your body. Some things don't work well with me. Dino doesn't work well with me. Deca doesn't work well with me. NP doesn't work well with me. I get really bad side effects. I get libido issues. I get high blood pressure, headaches, nose bleeds. And then the next person next door to me can take double what I'm taking and be absolutely fine.
