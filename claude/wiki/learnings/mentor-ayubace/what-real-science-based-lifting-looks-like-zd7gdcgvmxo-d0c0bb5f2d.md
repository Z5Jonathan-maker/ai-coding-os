# What REAL Science Based Lifting Looks Like

Terrence Ruffin who just won the Arnold and the New York Pro was training the show doing an upper lower split and he was doing like hamstrings and biceps. He's saying that because his hamstrings needed that. His biceps needed that. Phil Hernand who's now passed away trained the whole body every day. So he do like one exercise for chest, one for back, one for arms. Every day did that. And you know what? He had one of the best physics you'd ever see. All the professional bodybuilders have their own styles. Rather than listening to someone saying to you this is a scientific proof, try to experiment on yourself with different training principles and protocols and start to tailor your own training plan. Bodybuilding is science that you apply to yourself.
