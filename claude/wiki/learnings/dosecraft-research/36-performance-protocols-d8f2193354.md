# 36-performance-protocols

Performance compounds that work with your biology — not against it. Built on the Three Root Causes framework to ensure a foundation of health supports every performance gain.

| Compound | Primary Benefit | Dose | Cycle | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| MOTS-c | Metabolic optimization; fat burning; insulin sensitivity | 5-10mg SubQ, 2-3x/week | 12 weeks on, 4 off | Clinical |
| RAD-140 (Testolone) | Muscle mass; bone density; strength; libido | 10-15mg/day oral | 8-12 weeks on, 2-4 off | Experimental |
| Cardarine (GW501516) | Endurance; fat oxidation; cardiovascular efficiency | 10-15mg/day oral | 8-12 weeks on, 2-4 off | Experimental |
| 5-Amino-1MQ | NAD+ amplification; fat metabolism; metabolic rate | 50-100mg/day oral | Can run long-term | Expert |
| CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin | GH elevation; recovery; body composition | CJC 2mg/week; Ipa 100-200mcg 2x/day | As directed | Expert |
| BPC-157 | Injury prevention; recovery amplification | 500mcg-1mg/day SubQ | No cycling for acute injury; 8-12wk on / 2-4wk off for extended use | Clinical |

RAD-140 and Cardarine are **banned by WADA and most athletic organizations**. GH secretagogues are also prohibited in-competition. If you compete in tested sports, verify the status of every compound against your sport's prohibited substances list before use.

The endurance protocol leverages PPAR-delta activation and mitochondrial optimization to fundamentally change how your body produces energy during sustained efforts.

The strength protocol creates an anabolic environment through selective androgen receptor modulation and GH elevation without the androgenic side effects of traditional AAS.

If budget or simplicity is the priority, these three compounds deliver the most performance value:

"Reinforced Chassis"

Systemic repair. Joint protection. Recovery amplification. Uses the ATP surplus from MB to repair micro-damage from training.

"Nitrous Boost"

Metabolic performance. Fat burning. Insulin sensitivity. Channels energy to burn fat and boost output.

"Turbo Engine"

ATP production. Cognitive edge. Mitochondrial enhancement. Floods cells with energy for both brain and body.

Expert Synergy: BPC-157 uses ATP surplus from Methylene Blue to repair training micro-damage. MOTS-c channels energy to burn fat and boost performance. Creates a "closed-loop system of energy, metabolism, and resilience."

Body recomposition — simultaneously losing fat and gaining muscle — is the most sought-after outcome in fitness. Traditional approaches require choosing one or the other. Peptide stacking changes the equation by creating a hormonal environment where both can occur simultaneously.

| Compound | Dose | Role in Recomposition | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| MOTS-c | 5-10mg/week SubQ (2-3 injections) | Forces fat oxidation via AMPK activation. Shifts fuel substrate from glucose to fatty acids during exercise. Improves insulin sensitivity so nutrients partition toward muscle. | Morning, fasted, 30 min before training on workout days |
| RAD-140 | 10-15mg/day oral | Selective anabolic effect on muscle and bone without androgenic side effects. Preserves lean mass during caloric deficit. Increases training capacity and recovery speed. | Morning with first meal. Consistent daily timing. |
| CJC-1295/Ipamorelin | CJC 2mg/week; Ipa 100-200mcg 2x/day | Elevates GH for lipolysis (fat mobilization) and protein synthesis. Creates 24-hour anabolic environment. Improves deep sleep for recovery. | Ipamorelin: AM fasted + pre-bed. CJC: 1-2x/week SubQ. |
| 5-Amino-1MQ | 50-100mg/day oral | Inhibits NNMT enzyme, boosting NAD+ and metabolic rate. Increases resting energy expenditure. Accelerates fat cell turnover. | Morning with food |
| BPC-157 | 500mcg/day SubQ | Protects joints and tendons under heavy training load. Accelerates recovery between sessions, allowing higher training frequency. | Post-training or before bed |

Expert Recommended caloric intake during recomposition: maintenance calories (TDEE) or slight deficit (200-300 cal). Protein intake: 1g per pound of body weight minimum. The compounds handle the partitioning — you handle the nutrition.

Growth hormone is the master anabolic and lipolytic hormone. GH secretagogues stimulate your pituitary to release its own GH — maintaining the natural pulsatile pattern rather than flat-lining it with exogenous GH.

| Stack | Compounds | GH Effect | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | Ipamorelin 100-200mcg 2x/day alone | Moderate GH pulse; no cortisol or prolactin elevation | Beginners, recovery-focused athletes, women |
| Standard | CJC-1295 DAC 2mg/week + Ipamorelin 200mcg 2x/day | Sustained GH elevation (CJC extends half-life) + acute pulses (Ipa) | Most athletes; body recomposition; anti-aging |
| Advanced | Tesamorelin 2mg/day + Ipamorelin 200mcg 2x/day | Maximum GH output; Tesamorelin specifically targets visceral fat reduction | Athletes over 40; visceral fat reduction; maximum performance |
| Contest Prep | Tesamorelin + Ipamorelin + MOTS-c | GH + metabolic optimization for maximum leanness | Bodybuilding prep; photo shoots; competition peaking |

All GH secretagogues must be taken fasted. Food (especially carbs and fat) within 30 minutes of injection blunts the GH pulse by 50-80%. Wait at least 20-30 minutes after injection before eating.
