| **Janoshik** | Czech Republic | NOT accredited | $50-150 | Popular but no formal accreditation |

### What to Test Every Batch
1. Purity (HPLC) — must be 98%+
2. Identity (LC-MS) — confirms correct molecular weight
3. Endotoxin (LAL) — critical for injectables
4. Residual solvents — TFA, DMF, acetonitrile
5. Heavy metals — common Chinese manufacturing concern
6. Water content (Karl Fischer) — for lyophilized
7. Amino acid analysis — confirms sequence

### The Transparency Score Advantage
Your own brand naturally scores highest because:
- YOU control COA recency (fresh batch = fresh COA)
- YOU control third-party testing (Colmaric, every batch)
- YOU control purity threshold (reject anything <98%)
- YOU control shipping (domestic, climate-controlled)
- YOU control complaint response (instant)

The algorithm isn't rigged — your brand just wins on quality because you own the entire pipeline.
