# mitochondrial-primer

Why You're Tired and How to Fix It

The compounds, doses, and stacking strategies leading practitioners use to restore cellular energy production from the inside out.

ATP is the currency for ALL life functions. Every heartbeat, every thought, every cellular repair event requires ATP. Your body manufactures approximately **70 kilograms** of ATP every single day — roughly your entire body weight in molecular energy.

Mitochondria are the "nuclear reactors" inside every cell — thousands per cell in high-demand tissues like the heart, brain, and muscle. But these reactors are **battered, inefficient, and leaking toxic reactive oxygen species (ROS)** that damage the very structures they're supposed to power.

When mitochondria fail, everything fails. Not slowly — *catastrophically*. Fatigue, brain fog, slow recovery, metabolic dysfunction, accelerated aging. All roads lead back to the powerhouse.

Leading clinical experts identify three interconnected root causes behind virtually all chronic health decline. This guide focuses on Root Cause #3 — the energy crisis.

These three root causes are not independent — they form a vicious cycle. Mitochondrial dysfunction *drives* inflammation (ROS → NF-kB activation) and insulin resistance (impaired fatty acid oxidation). Fixing the power grid doesn't just restore energy — it breaks the cycle at its source.
