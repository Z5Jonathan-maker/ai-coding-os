# I&#39;ve been doing the same leg day for 20 years, here&#39;s why

My quads are probably one of my strongest body parts over the last 15 to 20 years. My routine has never changed. I've tried certain things. I've tried hitting the body part multiple times a week. I've tried switching exercises, but I always find myself going back to what works for my body. If you're changing things all the time, you never know what's going to work. If you have three or four good exercises that you connect with, stick with it. Get better with them, get stronger with them, change up the rep tempo, change up the volume from time to time, change up your intensity techniques from time to time, but you can keep the same exercise. I can do a leg press and do rests. I could do a drop set, right? I could do a cluster set. I can do partials, really slow reps, really fast. I can do so much with it. So sometimes when I'm going into a workout, I know I love this exercise. I know it works well with my body, but what I do with it can be dependent on many factors.
