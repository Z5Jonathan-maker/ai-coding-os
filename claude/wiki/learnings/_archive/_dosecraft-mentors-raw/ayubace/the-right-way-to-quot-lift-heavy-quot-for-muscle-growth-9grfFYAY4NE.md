# The Right Way To &quot;Lift Heavy&quot; For Muscle Growth

When I say use heavy weight with good form to build muscle, what I mean isn't comparing yourself to somebody else. It's using a weight which is challenging between the reps of 6 to 12. That's a good place to start. Where the weight is not so heavy you can't get six and a weight is not so light you just blast past 12, right? So you're kind of like when you get to that 12th reps, you're struggling, right? And if you feel like you go past it easy, then increase the weight. But the form is key because if you get stronger or your form is not as impeccable as it was on the first rep the warm-up set, you can use a lot of other muscles that you don't need to use to move that weight.
