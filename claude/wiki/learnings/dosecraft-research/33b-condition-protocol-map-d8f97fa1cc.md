# 33b-condition-protocol-map

"Start with the problem. Find the protocol. Follow the evidence."

9 categories — 35+ conditions — 40+ compounds — Evidence-graded protocols

Each entry maps to root causes, includes dosing, duration, monitoring, and contraindications.

This chapter is for educational purposes only. These protocols are not medical prescriptions. Always work with a qualified healthcare provider before starting any peptide protocol. Individual responses vary. Bloodwork monitoring is essential.

This chapter is the central decision engine of the entire Protocol Bible. When you have a health condition and want to know which peptides address it, start here.

Human clinical trials or extensive peer-reviewed research. Highest confidence.

Consistent practitioner consensus from experienced clinicians. Strong confidence.

Animal studies, in-vitro data, or early human use. Lower confidence, higher upside.

Every condition in this chapter maps to one or more of the three systemic root causes that drive virtually all chronic disease:

Chronic Inflammation

NF-kB, TNF-alpha, IL-6 cascade

Insulin Resistance

Metabolic dysfunction, glucose toxicity

ATP / Energy Shortage

Mitochondrial decline, NAD+ depletion
