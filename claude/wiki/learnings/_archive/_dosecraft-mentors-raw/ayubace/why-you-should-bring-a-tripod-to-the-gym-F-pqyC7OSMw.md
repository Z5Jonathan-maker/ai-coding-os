# Why You Should Bring A Tripod To The Gym

Record your session because what you think is going on in your head might not be actually how you're performing the exercise. When you look back at even myself, I'm like, "Fuck, no wonder I didn't really feel that. My form was actually off." Record one or two sets of your exercise and guess what happens? You're able to correct your form. You're able to monitor your progress on how you're lifting. Because if you're getting stronger, but your form starts to change, then is your muscle really getting stronger, or are you using other muscles to get that weight
