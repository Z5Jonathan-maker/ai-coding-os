# LOEkGQd_FXI_Can_You_Mix_Different_Peptides

I know a lot of people out there say you can't, but personally I have and it's worked absolutely fine. The way I see it is that this, it saves injection frequency. It can cause inflammation, it can cause pain, it can cause a little bit of scar tissue buildup. My opinion is you can mix your peptides because that's what I do and it hasn't hindered my results.
