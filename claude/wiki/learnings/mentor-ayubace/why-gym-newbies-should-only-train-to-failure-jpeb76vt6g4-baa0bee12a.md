# Why Gym Newbies Should ONLY Train To Failure

A newbie has gone to the gym fully loaded like action man. They're doing reps in reserve before they even know what their limits are. Are those three or four reps you're missing out on? Are they the ones that can actually change your physique? My advice to anyone watching this that is going to start working out, leave no reps in reserve. Because training to failure and training as hard as you can, you start to know where your limit is. You start to know how hard you can push yourself. Once you know where your limits are and you really have [ __ ] yourself by doing too much sets and too much reps, then you know where your reps and reserve are. But people don't even get to that point of feeling [ __ ] They're stopping themselves short. You never maximize your results.
