CRITICAL WARNING: ORAL BPC-157 DOES NOT WORK
Do not take BPC-157 orally. Despite marketing claims, oral BPC-157 capsules are destroyed by proteolytic
enzymes in the digestive tract and first-pass liver metabolism. The peptide is cleaved into inactive
fragments before reaching systemic circulation. The published studies showing BPC-157 efficacy all use
injectable (IP or SubQ) administration. Oral formulations are a waste of money — the bioavailability is near
zero for systemic applications.
"You cannot take a peptide orally and expect it to survive the GI tract intact. This is basic
biochemistry. If someone is selling you oral BPC-157, they're selling you expensive amino
acids."

250mcg per 0.1mL (10 units on insulin syringe).

means incomplete healing and higher re-injury risk.
