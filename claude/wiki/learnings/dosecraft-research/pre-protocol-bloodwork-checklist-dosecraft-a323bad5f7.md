TIER 1 — NON-NEGOTIABLE
The Essential Panel
These five markers form the absolute minimum. Do not start any peptide protocol without these results in
hand.
Fasting Insulin
< 5 mIU/L optimal  |  > 8 = problem
 INSULIN RESISTANCE
The single most important metabolic marker. Tells you if your cells are deaf to insulin's signal. Elevated fasting
insulin means GH secretagogues are contraindicated until this is fixed. Standard lab ranges go up to 24 — that's
metabolic disaster territory. Optimal is below 5.
MAPS TO ROOT CAUSE #2 — INSULIN RESISTANCE
CRP (C-Reactive Protein)
< 1.0 mg/L optimal  |  > 3.0 = active inflammation
 INFLAMMATION
Your systemic fire alarm. CRP rises in response to IL-6, the master inflammatory cytokine. Above 3.0 means your
body is running a chronic, body-wide blaze — and anti-inflammatory peptides should be your first priority, not GH
compounds.
MAPS TO ROOT CAUSE #1 — SYSTEMIC INFLAMMATION
IGF-1 (Insulin-like Growth Factor 1)
Age-dependent  |  baseline needed for GH protocols
ATP / GROWTH
The downstream marker of growth hormone output. Without a baseline IGF-1, you have zero way to know if your
GH secretagogue is actually doing anything. This is the before-and-after proof marker for every Ipamorelin, CJC-
1295, or Tesamorelin protocol.
MAPS TO ROOT CAUSE #3 — ATP SHORTAGE / CELLULAR REPAIR
CBC (Complete Blood Count)
Standard reference ranges
BASELINE
Red cells, white cells, platelets, hemoglobin, hematocrit. Screens for anemia, infection, immune dysfunction, and
blood cancers. BPC-157 and TB-500 can influence angiogenesis — baseline blood counts are essential safety
data.
GENERAL BASELINE — ALL ROOT CAUSES
Comprehensive Metabolic Panel
