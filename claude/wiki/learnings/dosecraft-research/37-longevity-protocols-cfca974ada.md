Telomeres are the protective caps on chromosomes. Each cell division shortens them. When critically short, cells enter senescence (zombie cells) or apoptosis. Epitalon activates telomerase — the enzyme that rebuilds telomere length — offering one of the most direct anti-aging interventions available.

| Protocol Element | Details |
|---|---|
| Dose | 500mcg-1mg/day SubQ, injected before bed |
| Cycle Length | 10-20 days per cycle (5-10mg total per cycle) |
| Frequency | Every 3-6 months (2-4 cycles per year) |
| Secondary Benefits | Restores melatonin production (pineal peptide), normalizes circadian rhythm, improves sleep architecture, antioxidant gene upregulation |
| Monitoring | Telomere length testing (blood test) annually. Expect stabilization rather than dramatic lengthening. Prevention of shortening is the primary goal. |
| Synergy | Combine with DSIP (before bed) for sleep optimization during Epitalon cycles. The two compounds enhance each other — Epitalon restores melatonin production while DSIP promotes delta wave sleep. |
