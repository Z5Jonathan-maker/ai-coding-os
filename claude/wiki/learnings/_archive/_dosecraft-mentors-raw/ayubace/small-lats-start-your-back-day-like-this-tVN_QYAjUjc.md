# Small Lats? Start your back day like this..

At the beginning of your session, go to a pull-up bar. Before you start any lat exercise, do some pull-ups, whether they're assisted or body weight. Start to stretch and contract your lats like that. I guarantee if you start your lat session with pull-ups, you'll automatically get a better mind muscle connection than the exercise you're going to do. I feel like every body part that you train, not just your lats, there should be an exercise at the beginning that kind of activates it. It's not necessarily taxing. It's not necessarily a warm up. It's just getting that mind muscle connection.
