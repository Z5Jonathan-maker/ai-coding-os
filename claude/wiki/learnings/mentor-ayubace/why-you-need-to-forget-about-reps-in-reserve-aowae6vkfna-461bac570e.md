# Why You Need To FORGET About Reps In Reserve

I've got 50 kg dumbbells. I'm pressing them and I get to eight and I know I've got three or four more left to do. I know I can feel it and I just put them down. Those four reps I just miss out on every session, every exercise. What is the point in that? And guess what happens? You stop yourself because say you're going to train that body part in 3 days time and then you stop yourself again. So you never ever push the limits. You're always in your comfort zone. Get outside of your comfort zone and push the limits. Push yourself. Overtrain. When you first start out, overtrain. know what it feels like to be actually fatigued and [ __ ] because then you will know how to manage your fatigue through stopping one or two reps short of failure. But you don't ever know because you don't push yourself. That's the problem with a lot of people.
