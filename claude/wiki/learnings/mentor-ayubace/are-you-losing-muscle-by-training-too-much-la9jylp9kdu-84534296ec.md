# Are You Losing Muscle By Training TOO MUCH?

When you've got an extreme calorie deficit, the mistake some people tend to make is now they're adding in cardio and they're doing high volume training. So, they're training with high reps and high sets, which essentially is going to fatigue you massively, which will result in muscle loss. What you want to do is keep your volume low with your training. No intensifiers, no drop sets, no super sets, just straight sets. And I would say around six to eight total work sets per muscle group across the week will see you maintain your muscle tissue. That can either be done in one session or that could be split over two sessions. You want to do two working sets with a top set of like an 8 to 10 rep range and a back off set with a 10 to 12 rep range. That will allow you to keep your strength, but also keep in a rep range which is not going to cause you injury because remember you're walking around without the energy you used to.
