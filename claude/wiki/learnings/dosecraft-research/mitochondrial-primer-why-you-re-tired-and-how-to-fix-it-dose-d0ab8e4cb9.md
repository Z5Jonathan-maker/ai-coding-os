The Power Outage
ATP is the currency for ALL life functions. Every heartbeat, every thought, every cellular

The Cellular Economy
Mitochondria are the "nuclear reactors" inside every cell — thousands per cell in high-demand
tissues like the heart, brain, and muscle. But these reactors are battered, inefficient, and leaking
toxic reactive oxygen species (ROS) that damage the very structures they're supposed to power.
When mitochondria fail, everything fails. Not slowly — catastrophically. Fatigue, brain fog, slow
recovery, metabolic dysfunction, accelerated aging. All roads lead back to the powerhouse.

MOTS-c is encoded directly in mitochondrial DNA — one of the few peptides with its own

MOTS-c can cause paradoxical fatigue if mitochondria are already inflamed. Overstimulation of the

block. Mitochondrial preparedness is required before use. Start with membrane stabilizers (Methylene
Blue, SS-31) and antioxidant support before introducing MOTS-c.
