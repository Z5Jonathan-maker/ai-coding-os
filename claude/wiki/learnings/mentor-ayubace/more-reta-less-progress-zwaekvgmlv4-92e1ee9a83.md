# More Reta = Less Progress

Let's just say that I went from 2 millig to 4 millig and I saw more progress in my body, but the side effects doubled. [music] So, I was throwing up, my energy level started to drop because I wasn't eating enough food to perform in the gym, wasn't eating enough food to recover. Was it really worth the jump? Will my progress be that much better over a period of time when my body's in a constant stress state when I'm constantly feeling sick and tired. I understand it's human nature that well, if this is working, then more must be better. But in a lot of cases when it comes to supplementation, when it comes to anabolics, when it comes to peptides, more is never better. What is better is using enough that progresses your physique without any adverse effects.
