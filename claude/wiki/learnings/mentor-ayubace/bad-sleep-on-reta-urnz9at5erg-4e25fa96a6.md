This will translate into your digestive system. So your bowel movement would [music] become less and also what you might see is an escalation in resting heart rate which is a sign of the body being in a stressed state. So what is the way around this? Well, number one, you can take it to true tie dosage in the morning. Number two, [music] you can lower the dosage and number three, you can split that dosage up.
