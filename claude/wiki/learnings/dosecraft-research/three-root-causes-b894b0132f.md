# three-root-causes

Why your peptides aren't working — and the biological failures you need to fix *before* any protocol will deliver results.

Every claim in this guide is tagged to one of three evidence lanes so you always know the quality of the data behind it.

You bought BPC-157 for your knee. TB-500 for recovery. Maybe GHK-Cu for skin. But six weeks in, results are inconsistent. Sound familiar?

The conventional approach treats each symptom with a separate intervention. Knee pain gets a peptide. Sleep gets melatonin. Energy gets caffeine. Inflammation gets an NSAID. Each fix has side effects requiring another fix.

This is the **pharmaceutical merry-go-round** — and peptide users fall into the same trap when they skip root cause analysis.

A 2020 JAMA Internal Medicine study showed polypharmacy in adults over 53 produces cascading adverse effects. The same principle applies to peptide stacking without a framework.

Leading practitioners have identified three systemic failures at the root of virtually every chronic condition. Fix these first — then peptides become force multipliers instead of band-aids.

A silent, body-wide blaze. Your immune system is confused, exhausted, and dumping inflammatory cytokines (IL-6, TNF-alpha) onto every tissue.

Your cells have gone deaf to insulin. The lock is jammed with intramuscular fat. Sugar ravages the bloodstream, glycating proteins and shredding capillaries.

Your mitochondria are battered, inefficient, leaking toxic reactive oxygen species. Producing less energy = energetically bankrupt at the cellular level.

These are not independent conditions — they fuel each other in a vicious cycle:
