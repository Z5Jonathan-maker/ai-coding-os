# Why Your Training HAS To Match Your Personality

you should try multiple splits and it's not even from a physical standpoint. What about a mental standpoint? Do you feel pleasure out of training a muscle group twice a week? Do you mentally like that? Or you someone that just wants to go in and go all out? Personally for me, I like going all out on arms. So my enjoyment in the training, my passion for the training, my ability to go intense increases through the want to do it. The more you enjoy your training, the more you look forward to it naturally, the harder you're going to push yourself. So there is also that psychological aspect.
