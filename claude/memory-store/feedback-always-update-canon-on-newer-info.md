Founder directive (2026-07-09): "I want everything as excellent and as accurate. Every time we discover new, more recent and updated information I always want you to be updating that stuff." Applies to ALL canonical stores — mym-autotrader `vault/` + `_INDEX.md`, auto-memory (`reference-*`, `project-*`), the roadmap/playbook files, and any doc that downstream decisions read from.

**Why:** stale canon silently drives bad live-money decisions (e.g. a roadmap allocating accounts to a prop firm that has since banned automation). A correction that lives only in the chat transcript is lost the moment the session ends. The vault/memory ARE the decision inputs — if they lag reality, the system degrades.

**How to apply:** when a research lane / scrape / verification surfaces a fact that is newer or more accurate than what canon says — and it's verified (primary source or corroborated) — update the canonical file(s) in the same pass, cite the source + date, and sync the relevant `_INDEX.md`. Prefer editing the existing entry over appending a duplicate. For facts that flip a prior "approved/safe" tag to "banned/risky", treat the update as priority (safety-relevant). Do NOT silently overwrite a founder-authored number (e.g. his own roadmap assumption) — update it AND surface the change so he sees it.
