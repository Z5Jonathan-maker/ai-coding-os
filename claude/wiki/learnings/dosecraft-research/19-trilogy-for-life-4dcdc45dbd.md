These three compounds don't just coexist — they actively amplify each other. The output of one becomes the input for another, creating a closed-loop system of energy, metabolism, and resilience.

MB bypasses mitochondrial bottlenecks, dramatically increasing cellular energy production. This ATP surplus becomes raw material for the next two compounds.

Cellular repair is an energy-intensive process. With Methylene Blue providing surplus ATP, BPC-157's repair cascades — angiogenesis, collagen synthesis, nerve regeneration — run at full capacity instead of being throttled by energy scarcity.

MOTS-c activates AMPK, directing the metabolic machinery to oxidize fatty acids and restore insulin sensitivity. The excess energy from MB and the repaired systems from BPC-157 provide the metabolic substrate MOTS-c needs to function optimally.

The loop repeats — each cycle making the system more efficient.

The Trilogy is designed as a foundation, not a ceiling. After 3-6 months of stable, tracked results, you can upgrade to the Top 4 Forever by adding Thymosin Alpha-1 (for immune recalibration) and Epitalon (for longevity). The transition is seamless because the Trilogy's three compounds are already part of both larger stacks.
