Founder directive 2026-07-16: strategy-creation + backtesting platforms are labs that already ran
thousands of experiments — mine each for FOUR things: (1) backtest METHODS (lift what beats ours,
flag weaker), (2) WINNERS (count + actual rules → harvest as hypotheses), (3) capability GAPS (what
they do we don't), (4) DATA surface (free/cheap feeds).

**Proof of concept = Nebula** ([[reference-nebula-harvest]]): brother's MT5 genetic factory, mined
end-to-end — framework doc, 3 winners decoded (gold ICT-structure hypothesis for our rank-0 slot),
106k-row experiment log mined for meta-signal, free MT5 CFD feed identified.

**Unifies existing veins as instances:** [[project-mym-trader-tape-harvest]] (Myfxbook/Darwinex/
Collective2), [[reference-tradingview-pine-crack]], aligrithm corpus. Platform-mining is the general
case; QKMS-ROADMAP.md 2026-07-16 section is the canonical doctrine.

**Hard boundaries:** public / open-source / owned-licensed ONLY; read-only; NO DRM defeat, NO
owner-admin keys, NO scraping private user data beyond what the platform shares by design, NO resale.
**Re-validation law:** every harvested edge is a HYPOTHESIS → re-run on OUR honest fills through the
gauntlet. Their sim ≠ ours (Nebula optimistic 0-slippage sim proved it). Take the RULE, not the P&L.
Feeds [[feedback-corpus-as-brain-standing-directive]] + the season engine classify step.

**Targets (ranked by research-scout afaa5769, pending):** GP factories (StrategyQuant X, EA Studio,
Adaptrade, Build Alpha) · community/leaderboards (QuantConnect Alpha, Numerai, Collective2, Myfxbook,
Darwinex, TradingView) · open-source + community results (freqtrade, vectorbt, Qlib, mlfinlab,
Quantopian/Lean archive) · prop/signal marketplaces. Rank by edge-density × accessibility × data-freeness.
Pipeline: recon → read-only harvest → mine (4 questions) → hypotheses.jsonl → OUR gauntlet → season classify.
