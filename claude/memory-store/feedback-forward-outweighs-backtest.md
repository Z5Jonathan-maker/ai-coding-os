Founder standing directive (2026-07-17): **forward testing holds more weight than backtesting.** When forward evidence and the honest gauntlet disagree, forward wins. The gauntlet (backtest-based statistical certification — DSR/placebo/PBO) is a SCREEN for what to put on demo, NOT the final veto. Forward performance is what decides funded size.

**Why:** forward is out-of-sample by construction, on real fills, with no curve-fit or look-ahead — genuinely more trustworthy than any backtest. Recurring session theme: the profitable demo book vs the gauntlet killing cells; the founder is right that live forward results outrank the model.

**How to apply:**
1. Never permanently bury a forward-positive cell on a backtest kill — keep it taping on demo to accumulate forward sample (consistent with the ratified tape-veto: the demo book is the courtroom).
2. Forward's WEIGHT scales with SAMPLE. A thin streak (e.g. the 2026-07 Sim101 +$1,880 that's ~93% ORB5 + 2-3 outlier fills over only 19 sessions) is forward evidence but WEAK — don't treat every green number as proven. Strongest signal = forward-positive AND adequate sample. The rule is "forward > backtest," not "any green forward = truth."
3. Division of labor: gauntlet decides what to DEMO; forward decides what to FUND.
