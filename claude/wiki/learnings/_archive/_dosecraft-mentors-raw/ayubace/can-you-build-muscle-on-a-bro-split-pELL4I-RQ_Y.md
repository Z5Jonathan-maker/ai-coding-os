# Can You Build Muscle on a Bro Split?

A lot of people today seem to believe that you need to train a muscle group two to three times per week in order to see any gains whatsoever. So something like the bros split, which is you training a muscle group once per week, has now been completely demonized. Like if you do that, you will not see results. And the thing about it is this. I have done so many splits and the bro split definitely has a place. For example, I've done my legs doing a bro split. I've also done my legs on a pushpull leg split twice a week and my legs actually got smaller. They lost their pop and the measurements started to drop. But on the other hand, if you look, for example, at my chest, when I split that up into two sessions across the week, it seems to grow better. It seems to have a better look. It seems to stay fuller and actually get stronger doing it. So, some body parts do respond better to frequency training and some body parts don't. But you can't just make a broad statement and say that if you train a muscle group two, three times per week that you're going to get results because you could be selling yourself
