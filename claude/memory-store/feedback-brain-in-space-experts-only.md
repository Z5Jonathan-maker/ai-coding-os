When enriching the DoseCraft Coach brain (or any DoseCraft content), source ONLY from experts
**in our niche** — the enhanced-athlete / peptide / TRT / bodybuilding-contest-prep / biohacking
space — plus **in-domain peer-reviewed research**. Explicitly NOT generic clinical dietetics, a
"random doctor on dieting," registered-dietitian/USDA/food-pyramid-tier advice, or any out-of-domain
authority.

**Why:** DoseCraft's moat is depth in a niche the mainstream gets wrong. Generic clinical/RD diet
advice is exactly the low-value content the audience already distrusts; it dilutes the brand and the
brain's authority. Jonathan (2026-06-16): "make sure youre only accepting expert content from ppl in
the space. not a random doctor on dieting."

**How to apply:**
- Ground authored dossiers in DoseCraft's own niche course content (`apps/web/content/paths/*`) +
  the in-space mentor brains (Layne Norton / Lyle McDonald-caliber recomp/flexible-dieting, the
  enhanced-community voices) + the peptide-research corpus.
- Citations must be **in-domain and verifiable**: GLP-1/peptide trials (STEP/SURMOUNT/retatrutide),
  bodybuilding contest-prep nutrition (e.g. Helms 2014), testosterone/partitioning (e.g. Bhasin 1996),
  BPC-157/TB-500/GHK-Cu repair (Sikiric, Pickart), protein/RT sports-science meta-analyses (Morton).
  Reject a citation if it's a generic-clinical-diet source or you can't verify it resolves
  (no fabricated NCT/PMC-as-author forms — caught 3 such in the 2026-06-16 nutrition batch and fixed).
- QC every batch: grep for generic-clinical red flags (registered dietitian / food pyramid / MyPlate /
  dietary guidelines / balanced diet / food groups) in addition to the editorial-posture name/handle scan.
- Editorial posture still applies: in-space experts POWER retrieval but are NEVER named in public
  output — see [[project-dosecraft-cleanup-audit-2026-06]] / EDITORIAL-POSTURE.md. "In-space sourcing"
  governs WHO informs the content; the posture governs that none are named.
- The highest-value structural fix flagged by the 2026-06-16 audit: re-ingest the BROKEN diet-expert
  scrapes (mentor-layne-norton = sale pages / 0 teaching content; mentor-lyle-mcdonald = sales pages)
